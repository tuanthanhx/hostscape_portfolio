# Color Schema

Sample: Creative Service Website
Slug: `creative-service`
Mood: sharp, modular, design-forward professional

## Palette
- Primary: `#111827`
- Secondary: `#374151`
- Accent: `#14B8A6`
- Background: `#F3F4F6`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#0F172A`
- Muted Text: `#667085`

## Usage
- Use `#111827` for bold headlines, case-study frames, and dark contrast sections.
- Use `#14B8A6` for interaction points, pricing highlights, and progress accents.
- The system should feel flexible enough for future service and case-study expansion.

# Color Schema

Sample: Cafe Brand Story Website
Slug: `cafe-brand-story`
Mood: cozy, handcrafted, neighborhood brand

## Palette
- Primary: `#5C3A2E`
- Secondary: `#A47551`
- Accent: `#D9A25F`
- Background: `#FBF5EE`
- Surface: `#FFFDF9`
- Text: `#2C211D`
- Muted Text: `#78665F`

## Usage
- Use `#5C3A2E` for logo areas, headings, and menu navigation.
- Use `#D9A25F` for buttons, featured drinks, and loyalty callouts.
- Pair warm neutrals with product imagery to keep the brand personal and inviting.

# TASK.md

Build a standalone sample website for this folder.

## Sample Identity
- Title: Cafe Brand Story Website
- Slug: `cafe-brand-story`
- Type: Multi-page cafe branding website

## Objective
- Create a warm branded cafe website that introduces the shop personality, menu, and location.
- The page should feel local, friendly, and visually memorable.
- The final website copy should be written in Japanese for prospective Japanese clients and visitors.

## Inputs
- Read `../../AGENTS.md`
- Read `../../SAMPLE_SPEC.md`
- Read `../../CODING_RULES.md`
- Read `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`
- Read `./COLOR_SCHEMA.md`

## Output
- Create a 4-page static website
- Required pages:
  - `index.html` for the cafe story and visit entry
  - `menu.html` for drinks, sweets, and seasonal highlights
  - `space.html` for interior mood, seating, and community appeal
  - `access.html` for hours, location, and visit guidance
- Keep shared assets inside the sample folder
- Create `css/styles.css`
- Create `js/scripts.js` only if interaction adds clear value

## Audience
- Japanese local customers or travelers looking for a cafe
- Users interested in atmosphere and specialty drinks
- Mobile visitors checking access, hours, and menu highlights

## Design Direction
- Mood: cozy, handcrafted, neighborhood-friendly
- Let the brand personality come through clearly
- Avoid generic coffee-chain presentation

## Content Direction
- Use realistic Japanese copy
- Avoid placeholder text
- Position the cafe around story, flavor, and everyday comfort
- Keep the tone warm and approachable

## Required Sections
- Hero:
  Japanese cafe headline, short brand message, visit CTA
- Brand Story:
  Shop concept, roasting or ingredient philosophy
- Menu Highlights:
  Coffee, sweets, or seasonal items
- Space:
  Interior mood, seating, or work-friendly appeal
- Access and Hours:
  Location, opening times, and practical information
- Community or Trust:
  Reviews, local connection, or social proof
- Final CTA:
  Visit or contact section
- Footer:
  Navigation and store details

## Interaction Guidance
- Small hover effects or subtle reveal motion are acceptable
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- The site should remain simple and welcoming

## Visual Constraints
- Follow `COLOR_SCHEMA.md`
- Use accent color for featured menu items and CTA moments
- Keep the atmosphere intimate and handcrafted

## Success Criteria
- The page looks portfolio-ready
- It reads as a believable Japanese cafe website
- It works on mobile and desktop
- It can be reviewed using `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md` without obvious gaps
- The Japanese copy feels appropriate for a real cafe brand

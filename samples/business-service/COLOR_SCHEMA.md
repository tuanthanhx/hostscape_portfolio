# Color Schema

Sample: Business Service Corporate Site
Slug: `business-service`
Mood: credible, modern, structured, B2B

## Palette
- Primary: `#16324F`
- Secondary: `#3F6C8F`
- Accent: `#2FBF9F`
- Background: `#F4F7FA`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#15202B`
- Muted Text: `#66788A`

## Usage
- Use `#16324F` for headers, trust sections, and corporate data tables.
- Use `#2FBF9F` for inquiry CTAs and small conversion-focused elements.
- Keep layouts restrained and legible with white surfaces and cool neutrals.

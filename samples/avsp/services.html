Capabilities

AV integration services shaped around Crestron control ecosystems.

NEXXTAV supports the full lifecycle of high-performance environments, from system strategy and programming through commissioning, training, and post-installation support.

01

AV system design and planning

Front-end discovery, room typology mapping, hardware recommendations, and control sequence planning to reduce friction during procurement and commissioning.

  • Functional scope development
  • Signal path and equipment planning
  • Coordination with architects and IT teams

02

Crestron programming and UI strategy

Clean, understandable control interfaces for executives, operators, guests, and facilities teams with scalable logic that supports future room expansion.

  • Touch panel and mobile control interfaces
  • Room modes, scenes, and automation rules
  • Reusable programming architecture

03

Conference room and collaboration deployment

Boardrooms, divisible meeting spaces, training rooms, and executive briefing centers built for reliable switching, audio clarity, and simple user workflows.

  • Display and routing integration
  • Scheduling panel and occupancy logic
  • Hybrid meeting support readiness

04

Residential smart control

Premium home automation experiences covering media, lighting, shades, climate, and entry systems with a strong focus on ease of use for everyday living.

  • Whole-home scene design
  • Entertainment and cinema control
  • Lighting, shade, and comfort automation

05

Support, tuning, and lifecycle care

Remote diagnostics, performance reviews, interface updates, and on-site refinement after deployment so installed systems stay dependable over time.

  • Post-launch issue resolution
  • System optimization and retraining
  • Expansion planning for future rooms

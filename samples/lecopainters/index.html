How it works

A straightforward process from site visit to final handover.

  1. Walk-through and advice The job is reviewed in context, including surface condition, access, sequencing, and the finish you want to achieve.
  2. Detailed quote Leco outlines the scope, prep requirements, recommended systems, and a sensible path for timing and delivery.
  3. Measured execution The work is staged cleanly, communicated properly, and finished with attention to edges, surfaces, and overall presentation.

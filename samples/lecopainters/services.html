01

Interior painting

Interior painting is where poor preparation shows quickly. Leco approaches walls, ceilings, trims, stairwells, feature areas, and whole-home repaints with attention to protection, patching, clean cutting-in, and a finish that feels composed room to room.

  • Suitable for occupied homes and active workspaces
  • Walls, ceilings, trims, doors, and detailed feature areas
  • Clean staging and low-disruption sequencing

02

Exterior painting

Exterior work is planned around weather exposure, substrate movement, and the condition of the surfaces underneath. That means better preparation decisions, better product matching, and a longer-lasting result.

  • Weatherboard, plaster, masonry, trim, and joinery
  • Preparation-first systems for exposed elevations
  • Sharper street presentation with stronger protection

03

Roof painting

Roof painting is part presentation upgrade and part maintenance strategy. Leco treats it as a technical service that needs proper cleaning, preparation, and coating selection rather than a cosmetic add-on.

  • Condition review before coating work begins
  • Preparation and treatment matched to roof type
  • Durability-focused coating systems with cleaner street appeal

04

Plastering and surface preparation

Good painting relies on the surface underneath it. Leco includes patching, filling, sanding, plastering, and repair preparation where needed so the final coat sits on a cleaner and more stable base.

  • Surface correction before decorative work starts
  • Smoother visual finish and better coating performance
  • Useful for renovation work and tired repaint surfaces

05

Commercial painting

Commercial painting needs more control around timing, access, tenants, staff movement, and how the site looks while work is underway. Leco is suited to projects where presentation and coordination both matter.

  • Offices, retail, common areas, and tenancy improvements
  • Scheduling that respects access and daily operations
  • Professional presentation for customer-facing spaces

06

Property maintenance support

Not every job is a full transformation. Some clients need a painter they can call for staged refreshes, pre-lease work, targeted exterior upkeep, or maintenance-driven improvements across multiple properties.

  • Maintenance-minded advice for repeat work
  • Useful for managed portfolios and staged upgrades
  • Practical for pre-sale, pre-lease, and in-between refreshes

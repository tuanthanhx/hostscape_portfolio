Before the quote

The more context the enquiry includes, the more useful the next conversation can be.

Good enquiries do not need to be long. They just need enough information to identify the property type, surfaces involved, timing, access constraints, and whether the job is mainly presentation-driven or maintenance-led.

Practical next step

After reviewing the enquiry, Leco can confirm likely scope, identify whether a site visit is needed, and outline the quoting path based on access, timing, and project complexity.

Why enquire early

Better painting outcomes usually start with better scoping.

Early discussions make it easier to identify repairs, access issues, surface condition, and the level of finish the property really needs. That leads to a cleaner quote and a smoother programme once work begins.

  • Helps define the right level of preparation
  • Reduces surprises around access and timing
  • Improves quote accuracy for larger scopes
  • Useful for both one-off and ongoing property work

Do you handle both homes and commercial sites?

Yes. Leco works across residential repainting, roof restoration, commercial spaces, and ongoing maintenance-led work.

Can I ask about work outside central Auckland?

Yes. Auckland is the core base, with select work considered further afield when the scope and programme suit.

What makes a quote easier to prepare?

Photos, property type, access notes, timing, and any known repair or surface issues help the quoting process move faster.

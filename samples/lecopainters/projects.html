Weatherboard family home repaint

Scope

Challenge

Outcome

Commercial interior | Newmarket

Office refresh delivered around business hours

A staged interior repaint across reception, meeting rooms, and circulation areas, planned to refresh the workplace without making the office feel like an active worksite all week.

Scope
Walls, trims, shared areas, and client-facing rooms
Challenge
Maintaining access while preserving a professional environment
Outcome
Sharper interior presentation with minimal business disruption

Roof coating | Takapuna

Roof restoration with a maintenance-first brief

This project centred on extending the life of a tired roof while also improving the way the property presented from the street after years of visible wear.

Scope
Cleaning, prep, treatment, and recoating
Challenge
Surface fatigue and inconsistent appearance from exposure
Outcome
Renewed protection and a cleaner, more resolved roofline

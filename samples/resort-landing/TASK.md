# TASK.md

Build a standalone sample website for this folder.

## Sample Identity
- Title: Resort Landing Experience
- Slug: `resort-landing`
- Type: Premium resort landing page

## Objective
- Create a visually immersive resort website that sells relaxation, scenery, and a premium getaway.
- The page should guide visitors from atmosphere and facilities to reservation intent.
- The final website copy should be written in Japanese for prospective Japanese clients and guests.

## Inputs
- Read `../../AGENTS.md`
- Read `../../SAMPLE_SPEC.md`
- Read `../../CODING_RULES.md`
- Read `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`
- Read `./COLOR_SCHEMA.md`

## Output
- Create a single-page static landing website centered on `index.html`
- Keep the experience immersive and conversion-oriented rather than splitting it into multiple pages
- Create `css/styles.css`
- Create `js/scripts.js` only if interaction adds clear value

## Audience
- Couples and families planning a resort vacation
- Japanese travelers seeking an upscale tropical or coastal escape
- Mobile users comparing resort options quickly

## Design Direction
- Mood: tropical, airy, premium, restorative
- Emphasize scenery, open space, and a sense of arrival
- Use elegant layouts rather than a crowded booking-engine style

## Content Direction
- Use realistic Japanese copy
- Avoid placeholder text
- Position the resort around nature, hospitality, and memorable on-site experiences
- Keep the tone aspirational but natural for Japanese travel marketing

## Required Sections
- Hero:
  Japanese headline, short resort value proposition, booking CTA, secondary discovery CTA
- Resort Overview:
  Atmosphere, landscape, and stay concept
- Guest Rooms or Villas:
  3 accommodation highlights with short descriptions
- Facilities:
  Pool, spa, dining, beach access, or activity offerings
- Local Experiences:
  Optional excursions or seasonal attractions nearby
- Guest Trust:
  Reviews, awards, or reassurance points
- Reservation CTA:
  Closing section encouraging booking or inquiry
- Footer:
  Access, contact-style details, and navigation

## Interaction Guidance
- Soft reveal motion or refined hover interaction is acceptable
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Avoid loud carousels or distracting gimmicks

## Visual Constraints
- Follow `COLOR_SCHEMA.md`
- Keep the page bright and breathable
- Use the accent color mainly for reservation actions and key highlights

## Success Criteria
- The page looks portfolio-ready
- It reads as a believable premium resort website
- It works on mobile and desktop
- It can be reviewed using `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md` without obvious gaps
- The Japanese copy feels appropriate for a real resort website

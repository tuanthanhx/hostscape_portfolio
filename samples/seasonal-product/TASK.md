# TASK.md

Build a standalone sample website for this folder.

## Sample Identity
- Title: Seasonal Product Promotion Page
- Slug: `seasonal-product`
- Type: Retail campaign landing page

## Objective
- Create a compact campaign page promoting seasonal products with urgency and clarity.
- The page should drive attention to featured items and limited-time offers.
- The final website copy should be written in Japanese for prospective Japanese clients and shoppers.

## Inputs
- Read `../../AGENTS.md`
- Read `../../SAMPLE_SPEC.md`
- Read `../../CODING_RULES.md`
- Read `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`
- Read `./COLOR_SCHEMA.md`

## Output
- Create a single-page static campaign website centered on `index.html`
- Keep the promotional message compact, urgent, and easy to scan in one flow
- Create `css/styles.css`
- Create `js/scripts.js` only if interaction adds clear value

## Audience
- Japanese users browsing campaign or seasonal products
- Mobile shoppers looking for quick product comparisons
- Users motivated by time-limited offers

## Design Direction
- Mood: bright, punchy, campaign-led
- Keep the page compact, energetic, and easy to scan
- The interface should feel promotional without becoming cluttered

## Content Direction
- Use realistic Japanese copy
- Avoid placeholder text
- Focus on product appeal, campaign timing, and purchase motivation
- Keep messaging concise and retail-friendly

## Required Sections
- Hero:
  Japanese campaign headline, promo period, primary CTA
- Featured Products:
  3 highlighted items with short benefit copy
- Seasonal Appeal:
  Storytelling around flavor, design, or use case
- Offer Details:
  Price, campaign terms, or purchase advantages
- Customer Reassurance:
  Shipping, support, or review-style trust points
- Final CTA:
  Purchase or campaign action section
- Footer:
  Basic navigation and campaign notes

## Interaction Guidance
- Product hover feedback or subtle reveal motion is acceptable
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Keep promotional clarity stronger than animation

## Visual Constraints
- Follow `COLOR_SCHEMA.md`
- Use accent color for discount and CTA emphasis
- Maintain clean hierarchy despite the high-energy style

## Success Criteria
- The page looks portfolio-ready
- It reads as a believable seasonal product campaign page
- It works on mobile and desktop
- It can be reviewed using `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md` without obvious gaps
- The Japanese copy feels appropriate for a real retail campaign

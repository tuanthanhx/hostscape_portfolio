# Color Schema

Sample: Seasonal Product Promotion Page
Slug: `seasonal-product`
Mood: retail campaign, bright, punchy, promotional

## Palette
- Primary: `#B23A48`
- Secondary: `#F08A5D`
- Accent: `#FFD166`
- Background: `#FFF8F4`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#2B1E22`
- Muted Text: `#7B6770`

## Usage
- Use `#B23A48` for campaign headers, ribbons, and major CTA areas.
- Use `#FFD166` to spotlight discounts, tags, and limited-time messaging.
- Let the page feel energetic without becoming cluttered.

Inquiry guidance

The strongest inquiries are simple, specific, and goal-based.

Mention your target

Say whether your focus is everyday English, an exam score, business communication, or junior study support.

Share your timeline

A rough start date or study period helps narrow program recommendations much faster.

Ask about student life

Campus routines, accommodation, and support matter as much as the classes for many learners.

Common questions

Prospective students and parents usually want clarity on the same key topics.

Which program should I ask about first?

Start with your main goal. That matters more than choosing the exact course name on your own.

Can I ask about campus life in the same inquiry?

Yes. Program fit and campus comfort should be considered together, especially for international students.

Is this page useful for parents too?

Yes. Parents comparing junior or family options should use the form to ask about support, routines, and safety expectations.

Before you send

Take one more look at the programs and campus experience if you want your inquiry to be more specific.

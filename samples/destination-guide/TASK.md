# TASK.md

Build a standalone sample website for this folder.

## Sample Identity
- Title: Destination Guide Portal
- Slug: `destination-guide`
- Type: Area guide and tourism portal

## Objective
- Create a portal-style website introducing a destination through highlights, categories, and seasonal appeal.
- The page should help users discover places and continue exploring.
- The final website copy should be written in Japanese for prospective Japanese clients and travelers.

## Inputs
- Read `../../AGENTS.md`
- Read `../../SAMPLE_SPEC.md`
- Read `../../CODING_RULES.md`
- Read `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`
- Read `./COLOR_SCHEMA.md`

## Output
- Create `index.html`
- Create `css/styles.css`
- Create `js/scripts.js` only if interaction adds clear value

## Audience
- Japanese travelers researching a destination before visiting
- Users interested in sightseeing, food, and seasonal recommendations
- Mobile visitors looking for curated highlights quickly

## Design Direction
- Mood: informative, exploratory, map-friendly
- The page should handle denser information without feeling heavy
- Favor structure and discovery over promotional hype

## Content Direction
- Use realistic Japanese copy
- Avoid placeholder text
- Present the area through categories, highlights, and travel utility
- Keep the tone editorial, helpful, and approachable

## Required Sections
- Hero:
  Japanese destination headline, overview copy, explore CTA
- Category Navigation:
  Food, nature, culture, stay, or shopping
- Featured Spots:
  3 to 6 location highlights
- Seasonal Highlights:
  Events or best times to visit
- Model Course or Travel Tips:
  Suggested route or travel advice
- Trust and Utility:
  Access, maps, or planning support
- Final CTA:
  Continue exploring or inquiry section
- Footer:
  Portal navigation and basic information

## Interaction Guidance
- Anchor navigation, tabs, or category filtering are acceptable
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Interaction should support navigation, not distract from it

## Visual Constraints
- Follow `COLOR_SCHEMA.md`
- Use accent color for highlighted spots and exploration prompts
- Keep dense content organized and readable

## Success Criteria
- The page looks portfolio-ready
- It reads as a believable Japanese destination portal
- It works on mobile and desktop
- It can be reviewed using `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md` without obvious gaps
- The Japanese copy feels appropriate for a real guide website

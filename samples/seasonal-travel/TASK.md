# TASK.md

Build a standalone sample website for this folder.

## Sample Identity
- Title: Seasonal Travel Promotion
- Slug: `seasonal-travel`
- Type: Travel campaign microsite

## Objective
- Create a campaign-driven travel page promoting limited seasonal trips, destinations, or packages.
- The page should make offers easy to scan and encourage fast action.
- The final website copy should be written in Japanese for prospective Japanese clients and travelers.

## Inputs
- Read `../../AGENTS.md`
- Read `../../SAMPLE_SPEC.md`
- Read `../../CODING_RULES.md`
- Read `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`
- Read `./COLOR_SCHEMA.md`

## Output
- Create a single-page static microsite centered on `index.html`
- Keep seasonal offers and booking prompts on one page for campaign clarity
- Create `css/styles.css`
- Create `js/scripts.js` only if interaction adds clear value

## Audience
- Japanese users browsing short-term travel deals
- Mobile-first travelers comparing plans quickly
- Users interested in seasonal highlights and package value

## Design Direction
- Mood: energetic, editorial, campaign-oriented
- Prioritize fast scanning, offer clarity, and visible CTA placement
- The page should feel lightweight and timely

## Content Direction
- Use realistic Japanese copy
- Avoid placeholder text
- Highlight timing, featured destinations, and booking benefits
- Use concise campaign-style messaging suitable for Japanese users

## Required Sections
- Hero:
  Seasonal campaign headline, campaign period, primary CTA
- Featured Offers:
  3 travel plans or promotional packages
- Destination Highlights:
  Attractions, scenery, or local appeal
- Why Book Now:
  Benefits, pricing advantages, or convenience points
- Travel Support:
  FAQ-style reassurance or service support details
- Final CTA:
  Strong booking or inquiry section
- Footer:
  Basic navigation and campaign information

## Interaction Guidance
- Tabs, filter-style UI, or simple reveal effects are acceptable
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Keep interaction secondary to campaign clarity

## Visual Constraints
- Follow `COLOR_SCHEMA.md`
- Use accent color to emphasize offers and limited-time messaging
- Maintain strong contrast and readability on mobile

## Success Criteria
- The page looks portfolio-ready
- It reads as a believable travel campaign site
- It works on mobile and desktop
- It can be reviewed using `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md` without obvious gaps
- The Japanese copy feels appropriate for a real seasonal campaign

# Color Schema

Sample: Seasonal Travel Promotion
Slug: `seasonal-travel`
Mood: energetic, editorial, campaign-driven

## Palette
- Primary: `#145DA0`
- Secondary: `#6DA9E4`
- Accent: `#FF7A45`
- Background: `#F7FAFE`
- Surface: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#10243A`
- Muted Text: `#627287`

## Usage
- Use `#145DA0` to anchor headlines, tabs, and travel category labels.
- Use `#FF7A45` for limited offers, buttons, and countdown-related details.
- Maintain high contrast and ample white space for mobile-first scanning.

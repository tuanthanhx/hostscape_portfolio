# TASK.md

Build a standalone sample website for this folder.

## Sample Identity
- Title: Compact Brand Presentation Site
- Slug: `compact-brand`
- Type: One-page brand presentation website

## Objective
- Create a concise one-page website for a small brand, campaign, or service launch.
- The page should feel fast, stylish, and focused on essential messaging.
- The final website copy should be written in Japanese for prospective Japanese clients and users.

## Inputs
- Read `../../AGENTS.md`
- Read `../../SAMPLE_SPEC.md`
- Read `../../CODING_RULES.md`
- Read `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md`
- Read `./COLOR_SCHEMA.md`

## Output
- Create a single-page static website centered on `index.html`
- Keep the experience intentionally compact rather than expanding it into multiple pages
- Create `css/styles.css`
- Create `js/scripts.js` only if interaction adds clear value

## Audience
- Japanese users landing from ads, social media, or campaigns
- Visitors needing a quick understanding of the brand
- Mobile-first users likely to scroll a compact page

## Design Direction
- Mood: concise, stylish, launch-ready
- Keep the page tight and intentional
- Every section should justify its presence

## Content Direction
- Use realistic Japanese copy
- Avoid placeholder text
- Focus on one clear message, supporting points, and a direct CTA
- Keep the tone modern and concise

## Required Sections
- Hero:
  Japanese launch headline, short supporting copy, CTA
- About or Message:
  Brand summary and positioning
- Key Features:
  3 selling points or highlights
- Visual or Social Proof:
  Showcase, testimonials, or trust markers
- CTA Section:
  Strong conversion block
- Footer:
  Minimal navigation and brand info

## Interaction Guidance
- Light reveal effects or anchored scrolling are acceptable
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`
- Keep interaction minimal and efficient

## Visual Constraints
- Follow `COLOR_SCHEMA.md`
- Use accent color for CTA and strategic emphasis only
- Preserve a clean one-page rhythm

## Success Criteria
- The page looks portfolio-ready
- It reads as a believable Japanese one-page brand site
- It works on mobile and desktop
- It can be reviewed using `../../REVIEW_CHECKLIST.md` without obvious gaps
- The Japanese copy feels appropriate for a real compact launch page
